A collection of some of my art projects. My medium is mostly in the digital field, although I still do the occasional traditional piece from time to time. The program I use for my digital art is FireAlpaca.

Titled "Winners POV", the digital drawing on the left consists of the characters created by my friends and I, which we use when we play games together. From top to bottom, the characters are Ein, Silver, Grunter, and Lemon.

The center piece was made for an assignment for ISU's CTK201 class. The purpose of the assignment was to combine, or make a remix on an art style. I chose to make mine based off of the style of Andy Warhol, who is famously known for his popart renditions of Camppells soup cans. Using my own take on the popart style, I combined the classic ghosts from Pac-Man with Warhol's style to come up with this project.

While most of my art is digital drawings, the piece to the right was heavily inspired by the 8-bit pixel art of games such as Stardew Valley, and Terraria. I used an online pixel art site to create this mini pixel sprite of my character, Silver.
