The two videos below are an example of my sound design work, and my arrangement work. The video to the left is a complete audio remake of the teaser trailer for the Disney animated film Big Hero 6. This was made by removing the audio from the trailer, and using audio sources and music to recreate the trailer in my own way. This was created using Adobe Audition for ISU's CTK340 Sound Design 2 class.

The video to the right is a string quartet arrangement of the song Jingle Bell Rock. I am part of a string quartet through my church, and I do majority of the arrangements for the quartet now. My favorite pieces I've arranged have been Jingle Bell Rock, Carol of the Bells, and pretty much any Christmas song I've been asked to arrange. This was created using the program MuseScore for Christ Community Church.
